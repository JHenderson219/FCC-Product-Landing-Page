2D glasses were originally devised by Hank Green for his wife, Katherine. Like many others, Katherine suffered from terrible headaches when viewing 3D movies. Being a fan of going to the movies with his wife, Hank didn't like this. So, Hank decided he needed to do something to bring this suffering to an end.
